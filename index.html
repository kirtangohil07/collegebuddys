Description

Having trouble managing your college work like to dos, assignments, notices etc. by different softwares, CollegeBuddy brings all to the one place from your time table to managing your assignment work CollegeBuddy has it all. Teachers can maintain record generate results of student upload their timetable and can check student performance. Students can see their results uploaded by their teachers and upload their assignments. It will also show growth report in overall semester of a student. It also has a To-Do list function to manage all your daily works for both teacher and student.
